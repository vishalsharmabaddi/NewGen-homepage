## Section 3 — Why People Switch (5 Reasons)

**Heading:** The 5 reasons homeowners choose a heat pump

**1) You want lower running costs**
Heat pumps are built for efficiency — using far less energy than old electric or gas systems, which can significantly cut your hot water costs over time.

**2) You want to make better use of your solar**
If you've got solar, a heat pump is one of the smartest ways to convert that daytime generation into something you use every day: hot water.

**3) You want to get off gas (or move closer to it)**
Many households are sick of gas bills and supply charges. A heat pump can be a practical step toward an all-electric home.

**4) You want to take advantage of rebates while they're available**
Heat pump upgrades can qualify for rebates and incentives that reduce the upfront cost — and many homeowners want to act while these are still accessible.

**5) You want to reduce your environmental impact**
Heat pumps are a cleaner way to heat water — and combined with solar, they can dramatically reduce emissions compared to gas.
