SECTION: Why Melbourne Homeowners Are Making the Switch
Intro: Still running an old gas or electric hot water system? Here are 5 compelling reasons why thousands of Melbourne households have already upgraded to a heat pump — and why you should too.

1. Save Significantly on Energy Bills
Hot water accounts for 15–30% of a typical Australian household's total energy use. Heat pump systems use up to 80% less electricity than conventional electric systems, and dramatically outperform gas on running costs. That's a real, measurable saving on every energy bill — month after month.
2. Maximise the Value of Your Solar
If you already have rooftop solar, a heat pump is the perfect partner. While a typical Melbourne solar system produces around 25 kWh per day, a heat pump consumes as little as 2–3 kWh. We can program your system to run during daylight hours, so your solar is doing the heavy lifting — at no extra cost to you.
3. Claim Generous Government Rebates
Victorian homeowners can access up to three separate rebates and upfront discounts:
Small-scale Renewable Energy Scheme (SRES): Save approximately $1,000 on eligible heat pump installations
Victorian Energy Upgrades (VEU): Point-of-sale discounts for eligible households — no waiting for reimbursement
Solar Victoria Hot Water Rebate: Additional support for eligible low-income households
As a VEU-accredited provider, NewGen handles the rebate process for you — you just enjoy the savings.
4. Get Off Gas Permanently
Gas prices have risen sharply over the past several years, and the trend shows no sign of reversing. An all-electric heat pump system frees you from gas bills entirely — no daily supply charges, no price volatility. Many Melbourne households are now saving $400–$800 per year after switching.
5. Better for Melbourne, Better for the Planet
Heat pumps produce significantly lower emissions than gas or standard electric systems. And when paired with rooftop solar, your hot water production becomes nearly zero-emission. It's a meaningful step toward a cleaner home — without compromising comfort.

SECTION: FAQs
H2: Frequently Asked Questions About Hot Water Heat Pumps

Do heat pumps work in Melbourne's winter? Yes — absolutely. Quality heat pump systems are engineered to operate efficiently even in cold temperatures. Melbourne winters are well within the operating range of the brands we install, including Sanden and Stiebel Eltron, both of which perform reliably down to very low ambient temperatures. We'll recommend the specific model best suited to your home size, household usage, and local climate.

Can I run my heat pump off my rooftop solar? Yes, and we highly recommend it. Heat pumps consume a modest amount of electricity — typically 2–3 kWh per day — which is a fraction of the output of a standard Melbourne solar system. We can program your heat pump to operate during peak solar hours (generally 10am–3pm), so you're heating your water with free solar energy rather than grid power. This combination can reduce your hot water running cost to near zero.

How much rebate will I receive? The amount depends on your household's eligibility and the product you choose. As a general guide:
SRES: Most households save approximately $1,000 on a qualifying heat pump installation
VEU: An upfront point-of-sale discount applied at the time of installation — no waiting
Solar Victoria: Additional rebates available for eligible low-income households
We'll confirm your exact entitlements during your free consultation and handle all paperwork on your behalf.

How long does installation take? Most hot water heat pump installations are completed in a single day. More complex installations — such as those requiring new electrical work, or replacing systems in difficult-to-access locations — may take slightly longer. We'll give you a clear timeline before we begin.

Will you remove my old hot water system? Yes. Our team will safely disconnect, remove, and dispose of your existing hot water system as part of the installation. You won't need to arrange anything separately.

What happens if my heat pump needs servicing after installation? NewGen provides ongoing support after installation. All systems come with manufacturer warranties, and we maintain relationships with the technical teams of every brand we install. If something isn't right, contact us directly and we'll sort it out promptly.

Which suburbs in Melbourne do you service? We service all of metropolitan Melbourne and many regional Victorian locations, including the eastern suburbs, northern suburbs, Mornington Peninsula, Geelong, Ballarat, and surrounding areas. Call us on 1300 878 888 to confirm availability in your area.
